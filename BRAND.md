# Moroccan Court Kings — Brand Foundation

This document locks the strategic decisions that every design, copy, and code change must respect. If you change something here, change it everywhere.

## Positioning (one sentence)

**Moroccan Court Kings — the 1v1 street basketball movement born in Marrakech.**

Long form: MCK is the first ever 1v1 basketball tournament in the African diaspora, built by a team of 30+ creatives, sports managers, photographers, and filmmakers from Marrakech.

## Slogan & tagline

- **Slogan:** *Pure Passion.* (used as the headline on the Story section and as a footer flourish — replaces the earlier "Basketball · Culture · Evolution")
- **Primary tagline:** *One court. One winner.* (used in the descriptor role under the logo / in meta description)
- **Secondary tagline:** *Marrakech-born. Diaspora-built.* (used in eyebrows or supporting copy where geography matters)
- **Declarative tagline:** *The first 1v1 in the African diaspora.* (used in the hero badge and SEO copy)

## Audience priority

1. **Players** — the people who compete. Everything funnels them to `Register`.
2. **Fans** — the people who follow. Everything funnels them to Instagram.
3. **Sponsors** — the people who fund. Everything funnels them to `Contact`.
4. **Media** — the people who cover. Press kit lives at `mailto:` for now.

The site must be navigable to all four within two clicks from the hero.

## Brand voice

- **Street, not corporate.** No SaaS-template phrasing. No "premier basketball tournament management system."
- **Confident, not boastful.** Claims are specific, not adjectives. "First ever 1v1 in the African diaspora" — yes. "Best basketball brand in Morocco" — no.
- **Moroccan-rooted, internationally-aimed.** Marrakech is the home. The diaspora is the horizon.
- **Active, not passive.** Verbs: compete, ignite, build, ship, push. Avoid: enable, leverage, facilitate.
- **Short sentences.** One idea per sentence. One sentence per line in copy.

## Language strategy

- **EN-only at launch.** Copy is intentionally short so French and Arabic translations can be added later cheaply.
- All copy is written so it survives translation: short, declarative, no idioms.

## Visual identity (locked defaults)

| Token | Value | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Primary | `#C42A2D` | Atlas red — hero accents, CTAs, headlines on cream |
| Surface dark | `#0E1A2B` | Deep navy — footer, dark sections, body text on cream |
| Surface light | `#F4EDE0` | Cream — page background, light sections |
| Accent | `#E0B354` | Ochre — sparingly for highlights, sponsorship CTA, micro-details |
| Sand | `#E3BEA4` | Warm peach-sand — polaroid frames, sponsor patch stitching, Story vertical rail |
| Ink | `#0E1A2B` | Primary text color (same as surface dark) |
| Muted | `#5C6470` | Secondary text |

**Typography:**

- Display: `Bebas Neue` (condensed sans, sport poster energy)
- Body: `Inter` (clean modern sans, excellent legibility)
- The previous `Sahara Scrolls` Gothic wordmark and `Cinzel` serif are deleted.

**Iconography:** `lucide-react` only. No emoji as UI icons.

**Visual motif:** subtle zellige-inspired geometric pattern as a single repeatable SVG used for hero backdrop and section dividers. Used sparingly so it stays a signature, not wallpaper.

## Naming standards

- Brand: **Moroccan Court Kings** (full) or **MCK** (initialism). Never "M.C.K." or "moroccancourtkings" as a brand spelling in copy.
